Top Picks: Our Favorite Games

A Guide to Gaming

Throughout our gaming journey from 2022 to 2024, we have curated a selection of three standout games from each year; each available on mobile, PC, and console. Discover the unique strengths of various platforms as we highlight the most captivating titles that have been released. Whether you’re playing on-the-go, immersively gaming at your desk, or experiencing gameplay on your console, there’s something for every type of gamer. Join us as we delve into the top games each year!

2022

Marvel Snapmore_vert

IOS/ Android

Marvel Snapclose

Marvel Snap is a digital collectible card game created by Second Dinner and published by Nuverse for Android, iOS, and Windows. It includes a variety of characters from the Marvel Universe. The game officially launched on October 18, 2022, following a beta testing phase.

Elden Ringmore_vert

PC

Elden Ringclose

Elden Ring is an open world action role-playing game released in 2022, developed by FromSoftware and directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki. It was launched on February 25 for PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Windows, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S. The game revolves around the quest to restore the Elden Ring and become the Elden Lord, facing various factions, mythical creatures, and powerful bosses along the way.

God of Warmore_vert

Playstation

God of Warclose

God of War Ragnarök is a 2022 action-adventure game developed by Santa Monica Studio and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It was released worldwide on November 9, 2022, for both the PlayStation 4 and PlayStation 5. The game continues the story of Kratos and his son Atreus as they navigate the challenges and consequences of Norse mythology.

The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom is a 2023 action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch. The player controls Link as he searches for Princess Zelda and fights to prevent Ganondorf from destroying Hyrule.
